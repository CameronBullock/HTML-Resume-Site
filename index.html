About Me

I have a passion for learning, tech and building things. In 2014 I received my bachelors degree in communication. I quickly went to work at a well established software consulting company, Construx Software. While my job at Construx focused on video production the glimpse I got into the world of development made an impact.

I started to teach myself web development. I attended conferences, scoured the internet for training and resources and I started building websites. I mainly focused on WordPress as a CMS to allow clients to maintain their websites after I built it for them. I learned to love building in WordPress I attend Wordcamps yearly and am involved in my local WordPress community. I continue to build and maintain WordPress sites with my company MotionWeb

I founded my own video production company called CourseBox. CourseBox focuses on online video training for Subject Matter Experts. I continue to build websites, Create online training with CourseBox and work to build and refine my skills.
